Team P1 - Challenge VI
Search Engine Optimization

  • Step 1: Create meaningful meta keywords. In the head of the html document, create meta keywords which include the keywords within the document that are important and consistent throughout the document.
  • Step 2: Create a meta description. The meta description which should also be located in the head of the document, must include what you would like to display when someone uses a search engine to find your page. What is provided in the description is what the user will see when the search is queried.
  • Step 3: The more pages the better your ranking will be. The amount of pages you have on your site is key to getting your ranking up. A site with a 100 pages with related content will have a higher ranking the 10 pages of related content.
  • Step 4: Find another site who is willing to add your link to their website. The more links to your site, within proper browser guidelines will increase your rank.
